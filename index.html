Pet Healthcare Guide

Dogs

Common Symptoms of Illness

Lethargy, loss of appetite, vomiting, diarrhea, coughing

Vaccination Schedule

Puppies: 6-8 weeks, 10-12 weeks, 14-16 weeks
Adults: Annual boosters

Cats

Common Symptoms of Illness

Changes in appetite, excessive thirst, hiding, changes in litterbox habits

Vaccination Schedule

Kittens: 6-8 weeks, 10-12 weeks, 14-16 weeks
Adults: Annual boosters

Rabbits

Common Symptoms of Illness

Reduced appetite, lethargy, abnormal droppings, dental issues

Care Requirements

Regular grooming, hay-based diet, exercise needs, dental care
